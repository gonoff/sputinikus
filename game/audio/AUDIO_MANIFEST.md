# Audio Manifest for The Chronicler's Device

This document lists all audio files needed for the game.
Place .ogg files in the appropriate directories.

## Music (game/audio/music/)

### Ambient/Background
- `ambient_rain.ogg` - Gentle rain ambience for opening scene
- `florence_ambient.ogg` - Renaissance Florence street sounds
- `florence_day.ogg` - Upbeat Florence daytime music

### Mood Music
- `tension.ogg` - Building tension/suspense
- `elegant_tension.ogg` - Sophisticated tension for dinner party
- `mysterious.ogg` - Mysterious/intriguing atmosphere
- `wonder.ogg` - Sense of awe and discovery
- `anticipation.ogg` - Building excitement

### Rome/Vatican
- `grandeur.ogg` - Grand, impressive themes
- `rome_grandeur.ogg` - Rome-specific grandeur
- `danger.ogg` - Approaching danger
- `tension_dark.ogg` - Dark tension for Inquisition
- `escape.ogg` - Urgent escape music

### Return/Endings
- `wonder_return.ogg` - Returning to transformed world
- `triumph.ogg` - Victory/legendary ending
- `melancholy.ogg` - Sad/fraud exposed ending
- `dark_fate.ogg` - Inquisition ending
- `bittersweet.ogg` - Limited impact ending
- `dark_power.ogg` - Corruption ending
- `chaos.ogg` - Paradox ending
- `quiet_regret.ogg` - Early return ending

## Sound Effects (game/audio/sfx/)

- `metallic_clang.ogg` - Shovel hitting mysterious box
- `box_opening.ogg` - Time machine box opening
- `time_travel.ogg` - Time travel activation sound
- `horse_neigh.ogg` - Horse sound for Pietro's cart
- `crowd_gasping.ogg` - Crowd reaction to eclipse

## Notes

- All audio files should be in OGG Vorbis format
- Music should loop seamlessly where appropriate
- Recommended bitrate: 128-192 kbps for music, 96-128 kbps for SFX
- If audio files are missing, the game will run but without sound
